BACKGROUND<br /><br />
<br /><br />
<br /><br />
(A) A Production Sharing Contract (hereafter referred to <br /><br />
    as the “Contract”) in respect of Petroleum <br /><br />
    Operations relating to the Ber Bahr Block in the <br /><br />
    Kurdistan Region of Iraq was executed on [ j 2009 <br /><br />

(B) Article 4.6 of the Contract provides for an option, <br /><br />

    Assignee wishes to accept such rights, duties, <br /><br />
    obligations and liabilities under and in the Contact <br /><br />
    in so far as they relate to the Assigned Interest; <br /><br />
    and<br /><br />
